30 Description
31  Standard k-epsilon turbulence model for incompressible flows.
32 
33  Reference:
34  \verbatim
35  Launder, B.E., and Spalding, D.B.,
36  "Mathematical Models of Turbulence",
37  Academic Press, 1972.
38 
39  Launder, B.E., and Spalding, D.B.,
40  "Computational Methods for Turbulent Flows",
41  Comp. Meth in Appl Mech & Eng'g, Vol 3, 1974, pp 269-289.
42  \endverbatim
43 
44  The default model coefficients correspond to the following:
45  \verbatim
46  kEpsilonCoeffs
47  {
48  Cmu 0.09;
49  C1 1.44;
50  C2 1.92;
51  sigmaEps 1.3;
52  }
53  \endverbatim

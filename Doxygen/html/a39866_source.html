30 Description
31  The choric Smagorinsky Model for compressible flows.
32 
33  Algebraic eddy viscosity SGS model founded on the assumption that
34  local equilibrium prevails.
35  Thus,
36  \verbatim
37 
38  B = 2/3*k*I - 2*nuSgs*dev(D)
39 
40  where
41 
42  D = symm(grad(U));
43  k from rho*D:B + ce*rho*k^3/2/delta = 0
44  muSgs = ck*rho*sqrt(k)*delta
45  \endverbatim

30 Description
31  Mixture k-epsilon turbulence model for two-phase gas-liquid systems
32 
33  The basic structure of the model is based on:
34  \verbatim
35  "Modelling of dispersed bubble and droplet ow at high phase fractions"
36  A. Behzadi, R.I. Issa , H. Rusche,
37  Chemical Engineering Science (59) 2004 pp.759-770.
38  \endverbatim
39 
40  but an effective density for the gas is used in the averaging and an
41  alternative model for bubble-generated turbulence from:
42  \verbatim
43  "The simulation of multidimensional multiphase flows",
44  Lahey R.T.,
45  Nucl. Eng. & Design
46  (235) 2005 pp.1043-1060.
47  \endverbatim
48 
49  The default model coefficients correspond to the following:
50  \verbatim
51  mixtureKEpsilonCoeffs
52  {
53  Cmu 0.09;
54  C1 1.44;
55  C2 1.92;
56  C3 C2;
57  sigmak 1.0;
58  sigmaEps 1.3;
59  }
60  \endverbatim

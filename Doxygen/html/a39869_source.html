30 Description
31  The Isochoric Smagorinsky Model for incompressible flows.
32 
33  Algebraic eddy viscosity SGS model founded on the assumption that
34  local equilibrium prevails.
35  Thus,
36  \verbatim
37  B = 2/3*k*I - 2*nuSgs*dev(D)
38  Beff = 2/3*k*I - 2*nuEff*dev(D)
39 
40  where
41 
42  D = symm(grad(U));
43  k = (2*ck/ce)*delta^2*||D||^2
44  nuSgs = ck*sqrt(k)*delta
45  nuEff = nuSgs + nu
46  \endverbatim
